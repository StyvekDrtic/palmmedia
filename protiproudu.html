project overview

Our main focus behind this project is to bring even more listeners to the already biggest podcast in the Czech Republic. We’re even trying to target people that have no idea what podcasts are. We’re in charge of producing additional content (highlights, photos, etc.) that is constantly being shared all across social media. We’re also helping with the social media strategy itself and the post-production of the actual episodes. That includes things like sound correction or cutting. When it’s needed - we also take care of additional graphic design tasks that are necessary for a podcast, this big, to be able to work.
