project overview

Podcasting is a great way to connect to your end-consumers on a deeper level. That's why marketers at Queens have decided to get into this medium. We helped them to rebuild their studio so they can record better quality audio, and incorporated some strategic changes into their strategy. We're also taking care of all post-production and audio development of every episode. One of the most valuable benefits of our cooperation is that we're able to process the whole episode within 48 hours. Speed is our top priority. Would you like to learn more about this project? Go listen to it on Apple Podcasts, Spotify or Soundcloud.
